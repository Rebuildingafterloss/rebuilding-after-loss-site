My Story

How I came to this work.

"You are allowed to grow around your grief."

My journey with grief began when I lost my dad at the age of 16. At a time when many of my peers were focused on school, friendships, and their future, I was learning how to navigate life after a significant loss.

In the years that followed, I experienced many of the challenges that grief can bring, including depression, anxiety, and a loss of direction. Like many people living with grief, I spent time searching for ways to move forward while carrying the pain of what had happened.

Six years later, I faced another profound loss when my mam was diagnosed with glioblastoma, an aggressive and terminal brain cancer. At 23, while completing my final years of college, I became one of her carers and supported her through the final stage of her life.

These experiences taught me that grief is not something we "get over." It changes us. Yet I also learned that while loss becomes part of our story, it doesn't have to define the rest of our lives.

Over time, I discovered the importance of facing grief rather than avoiding it, creating space for difficult emotions, and rebuilding a life that still held meaning, purpose, and hope.

These experiences became the foundation of the work I do today, supporting others as they navigate their own grief journeys.

My Approach

My role is to walk alongside you.

My goal is not to take away your grief or lessen the love you have for the person you have lost. Grief and love are deeply connected, and one does not exist without the other.

This journey is about helping you see that while loss changes your life, it does not end it.

Together, we will create space for your grief, honour the person you love, and explore what life looks like now.

My hope is that by the end of our time together, you will be able to see a future for yourself again, one that holds meaning, purpose, connection, and hope.

This is your journey, and you don't have to do it alone.
The Programme

The 12-Week Journey

A structured, supportive programme delivered in four blocks. Each stage builds on the last, helping you move forward with clarity, confidence, and self-compassion.

Weeks 1–3

Foundations

  • Creating a safe space to begin
  • Understanding your grief
  • Exploring your emotions
  • Building stability and self-care

Goal: To feel supported, understood, and more grounded.

Weeks 3–6

Growth

  • Understanding how loss has changed you
  • Reconnecting with yourself
  • Challenging limiting beliefs
  • Finding meaning and rediscovering this new version of you

Goal: To gain clarity, rebuild confidence, and rediscover what matters to you.

Weeks 6–9

Hope

  • Building hope for the future
  • Strengthening your mindset and resilience
  • Creating a life that feels right for you
  • Planning your next chapter

Goal: To support you in seeing what's possible and believing in your future again.

Weeks 9–12

Moving Forward

  • Taking meaningful action
  • Strengthening connections
  • Living with purpose and self-compassion
  • Embracing life while honouring your loved one

Goal: To step forward with confidence and purpose.
